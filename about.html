We have produced work in all fields of prop creation, including demonstration props for PETA, custom sign work for stalls at Glastonbury festival and bespoke items for weddings and events.
Focussing heavily in using sustainable/recycled wood, and traditional handpainting techniques.

We create items including backdrops, props, signs and designs straight from the client's imagination.
We work in Torquay, Devon. Our custom creations are sent all over the UK and abroad.
Laura, the main prop builder, has designed and made a vast array of props and signs, which you can view many of on the Gallery page.
We are now very excited to be offering an ever-growing range of signs, and rentable props to our local area of Torbay.
Renting is a great way to be sustainable, and by keeping it local we hope to add something extra to an event, without adding additional impact to our enviroment as much as we can.
With our range of skills available, be sure that we can also make anything extra and bespoke you may need for your event!

Please have a look at the Gallery and get in touch to discuss your project idea.
